About The Royal Light

The Royal Light, based in Doha, Qatar, delivers modern, high-quality lighting solutions. Our flagship brand, LORDEX (est. 2003), is renowned for innovative, durable products that combine design excellence with advanced technology.

We serve residential, commercial, industrial, and governmental projects with tailored solutions, expert consultation, and professional installation.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, innovative, and energy-efficient lighting that enhances spaces with superior design and long-term value.

Our Vision

To be the region's leading lighting solutions provider, recognized for excellence, innovation, and sustainability.
